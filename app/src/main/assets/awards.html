SAB Ke Anokhe Awards

Sab Ke Anokhe Awards is an annual Indian television award show. It is an award show which has "Anokhe"(Unique) awards from shows of SAB TV. It was first held in 2012. It usually airs in Mid-Summers and respects the characters of shows. Some of the awards include: Sab se anokha/anokhi: Museebat ka Maara, Naree, Parivar, Jodi, Rishtedar, Mehmaan Kalakaar, Padosi, Buddhu, Love Buds, Yaari and the biggest award is Sab Se Anokha Show which is won by Taarak Mehta Ka Ooltah Chashmah from 2012.Just like Film-Based award functions, it includes Awards as well as Performances of dance and comedy in between. The Hosts are mostly comedians.
